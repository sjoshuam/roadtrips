My Travels Across the United States of America

Years ago, I built my first simulation model of population dynamics in the United States. I got to know US places and population centers very well, realizing how few I had experienced in person. I set out on my first roadtrip with little more than a rough list of a few places and boundless curiosity. Since then, I have traveled thousands of miles, and evolved my ambitions. These days, my domestic travel goals include visiting:

  • Capital: Every state's capital city
  • State: Every state's most populous city
  • Population: The nation's most populous cities
  • Significance: Cities near noteworthy historical, cultural, or environmental sites

This information dashboard tracks my past progress towards achieving my goals and provides planning information for my future trips.
